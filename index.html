Installation

  1. Download a device type.
  2. Unzip the .simdevicetype.zip file.
  3. Move the extracted folder into:

/Library/Developer/CoreSimulator/Profiles/DeviceTypes

  1. Restart Xcode (If open) and Simulator.
  2. The device should appear in the simulator device list.
